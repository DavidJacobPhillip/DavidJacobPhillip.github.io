What was challenging about the assignment, and what specific kinds of problems did you have. How did you solve those problems?

The biggest challenges I faced were working with various new types of code, so figuring out how everything works was quite difficult. I solved these problems by looking through my notes and understanding how the teacher completed similar problems.

What did you learn from the assignment? Were there any special insights you had? What did you find that you already knew?

Most notibly, I learned how to use JavaScript to manipulate the HTML DOM itself; this particular connection was what I was most excited for going into this assignment. I had worked on JavaScript in the past, but hadn't gotten more complex than creating a few functions.

What kinds of resources were helpful for completing the assignment? Specific websites? Lectures? The class Piazza forum? The TAs? How did you use each of these resources?

Quite honestly, the most useful resource were my friends. Working together helps tackle issues that sometimes you cannot see on your own. For instance, I was stuck trying to figure out why my 'getElementsByClassName' wasn't working, and my friend noticed that I hadn't included an index. Without him, I may have spent countless minutes trying to figure it out!

What are one or two things you had to Google to complete the assignment?

There were a few coding elements I needed to google. Specifically how to use 'toUpperCase', which I implemented incorrectly within my program the first time around.
